3.      
THE RESULTS BASED INFOMERCIAL 
While the Testimony Informercial focuses on a story and may include results the 
Results Based Informercial only speaks of the result. There is very little story at the 
beginning. Go straight to the result. 
In this infomercial once again there is no time for preamble or saying what you do. You 
must leave that out entirely. Tell them the result you had with one person that is all. 
The ask is simple and it is never “Would you like to do business with me?” Take the 
onus off the person in front of you instead by asking “Who do you know that needs this 
result?”
