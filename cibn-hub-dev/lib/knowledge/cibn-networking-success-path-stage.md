CIBN Networking Success Path – Stage 1
Comprehensive Knowledge File for GPT Training (Stage 1 Only)
1. Testimonial Infomercial (Stage 1 Skill)
• Must fit within 45–60 seconds.
• Respect the timer at all times.
• Replace regular infomercial (do not combine).
• Structure: Name → Client Problem → Result → Who You’re Looking For → Name Again.
• Rotate 4–6 different testimonials over time.
• Do not explain how you solved the problem—only state the result.
2. Your Next 25 Connections
• Write down your next 25 connections (CIBN members and referrals from them).
• Track referral chains (who introduced whom).
• Record where every lead originates.
• Apply 7–14 touches through social media and follow-ups.
• Connect on LinkedIn and other platforms.
• Track return on investment (ROI).
3. Collaborators
• Same target market, different product line.
• Not a JV partnership.
• Built on trust over time.
• Start with one referral test.
• Build 3–5 collaborators for 3–5 leads per month each.
• Collaborators share active buyers.
4. Your Why
• Your Why is not your mission, values, or vision.
• Your Why is what drives you emotionally.
• If it sounds nice, it’s not deep enough.
• Use repeated 'Why?' questioning to go deeper.
• Stop when it creates emotional response (knot, tears, intensity).
• Share moderate vulnerability in one-on-ones.
• Ask others for their Why and dig deeper.
5. How to Get 10 Referrals
• Build referral asking into your process (contract or checklist).
• Never ask vague questions.
• Ask 10 specific referral questions.
• Separate name generation from contact collection.
• Sit with the person—never send sheet home to fill later.
• Specific prompts trigger names; vague prompts trigger silence.
6. Structured One-on-One Flow
• Prepare (research website and LinkedIn).
• Ask their Why first.
• Take notes (notebook or CRM).
• Share your Why and vision.
• Present your offer clearly.
• Clarify purchase vs referral ask.
• Ask specific referral questions.
• Track closing ratio (e.g., 2/10 = 20%).
• Use a consistent script or outline.
• Measure and improve performance.
7. How to Give 10 Referrals
• Referrals are gifts to three people: receiver, referred person, and you.
• Prepare before one-on-ones to give 1–3 trial referrals.
• Evaluate performance before giving more.
• Introduce collaborative partners as high-value referrals.
• Take notes and send follow-up introductions.
• Over time, arrive with 10 referrals ready.
• Generosity builds reciprocity.
