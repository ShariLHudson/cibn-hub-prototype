Topic: Inviting Up To 1,000 People To A LinkedIn Event AND Proper Follow-Up Process
Built ONLY from the transcripts provided. Cleaned. Structured. Expanded into procedural steps for GPT use.
SECTION 1: PURPOSE OF THE SYSTEM
• Fill webinars (Zoom-based events).
• Fill in-person local events.
• Fill LinkedIn broadcast-style events.
• Increase attendance using LinkedIn first-level connections.
• Convert LinkedIn 'Interested' clicks into actual registrations.
• Capture email addresses for CRM entry.
SECTION 2: HOW TO CREATE A LINKEDIN EVENT
Step 1 – Access Event Creation
• Go to LinkedIn.
• Click 'My Network'.
• Click 'Events' on the left panel.
• Click 'Create'.
Step 2 – Complete Event Setup Fields
• Upload event image.
• Write full event description.
• Choose event type: In-Person OR Online.
If In-Person Event:
• Enter physical location.
• Add event details.
• Include registration or payment link if required.
If Online Event:
• Option A: Use Zoom link directly (faster signup, higher Zoom bombing risk).
• Option B: Use landing page registration link (recommended).
• Landing page allows collection of contact information.
• Landing page supports CRM growth and email list building.
LinkedIn Broadcast Event Option:
• LinkedIn allows an audio-style event format.
• Cannot share screen.
• Primarily voice-based participation.
SECTION 3: INVITING UP TO 1,000 CONNECTIONS
Precondition:
• You must mark yourself as 'Going' to your own event before inviting others.
Step-by-Step Invite Process:
• Open your event page.
• Click 'Share'.
• Click 'Invite'.
• The CIBN Connection Extension window appears.
• Click to begin loading contacts.
• Optional: Filter by location for city-based events.
• Allow system to auto-populate connections.
• Click 'Stop' when desired number appears.
• Click 'Invite' to send invitations.
System Limitations Mentioned:
• Up to approximately 1,000 invites per week total across events.
• Requires LinkedIn first-level connections.
• Works best when user has 300+ connections (more connections = more reach).
SECTION 4: WHY FOLLOW-UP IS CRITICAL
• 'Interested' or 'Accept' does NOT equal registration.
• Users may click accept without reading details.
• Some users click accidentally.
• LinkedIn does NOT automatically move them to registration form.
• You must manually re-engage them.
Follow-Up Timing:
• Begin follow-up approximately 24 hours after invitations are sent.
SECTION 5: HOW TO IDENTIFY WHO TO MESSAGE
• Open event page.
• Click 'Networking' tab.
• Review attendees marked as 'Interested' or 'Going'.
• Identify FIRST-LEVEL connections (message button visible).
• Second and third-level connections may not allow direct messaging.
• Avoid excessive connection requests (LinkedIn may restrict account if overused).
SECTION 6: FOLLOW-UP MESSAGE STRUCTURE
Option 1 – Offer To Register Them (Higher Conversion Method)
• Message format:
• “Hi [Name], I see you’re interested in the [Event Name]. Would you like me to register you? All I need is
your email address.”
• Reported result: Approximately 25% higher conversion when offering to register them directly.
Option 2 – Send Registration Link
• Message format:
• “Hi [Name], I see you’re interested in the [Event Name]. This is the link to register: [Insert Link].”
Operational Notes:
• Copy and paste message format for efficiency.
• Customize first name only for speed.
• Repeat process through first-level connections list.
SECTION 7: TEAM MULTIPLIER STRATEGY
• Recruit 3–10 collaborators to assist with invites.
• Each collaborator sends invitations to their own 1,000 contacts.
• Each collaborator follows up with their own first-level connections.
• This multiplies total reach and attendance significantly.
Workflow Model:
• Day 1: All team members send invites.
• Day 2–3: All team members follow up with first-level connections.
• Capture emails into CRM.
• Repeat follow-up as event date approaches.
SECTION 8: CRM CAPTURE PRINCIPLE
• Always drive traffic to landing page when possible.
• Landing page collects email and contact data.
• Do not rely solely on LinkedIn acceptance.
• Email capture allows future follow-up marketing.
