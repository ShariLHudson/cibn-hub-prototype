# CIBN — Event Invites & Follow-Up System

## Overview

This file explains how to invite large numbers of LinkedIn connections to events and convert interest into actual registrations. The AI uses this file when users want to promote events, increase attendance, and improve follow-up results.

---

## Purpose of This System

This process is designed to:

* Fill webinars and online events
* Increase attendance at in-person events
* Convert LinkedIn interest into registrations
* Capture contact information for follow-up

---

## Step 1 — Create a LinkedIn Event

### Basic Setup

1. Go to LinkedIn
2. Click “My Network”
3. Click “Events”
4. Click “Create”

---

### Event Details

Include:

* Event image
* Clear description
* Event type (online or in-person)

---

### Online Event Options

**Option 1: Direct Zoom Link**

* Faster sign-up
* Higher risk of unwanted access

---

**Option 2: Landing Page (Recommended)**

* Collects email addresses
* Builds your CRM
* Improves follow-up opportunities

---

## Step 2 — Invite Up to 1,000 Connections

### Important First Step

👉 Mark yourself as “Going” to your event

---

### Invite Process

1. Open your event
2. Click “Share”
3. Click “Invite”
4. Use the extension popup
5. Load your connections
6. Apply filters (optional)
7. Select desired number
8. Send invites

---

### System Notes

* Up to ~1,000 invites per week
* Requires first-level connections
* Works best with larger networks

---

## Step 3 — Follow-Up (CRITICAL STEP)

### Why This Matters

* “Interested” does NOT mean registered
* Many users click without reading
* LinkedIn does not move users to registration

👉 Follow-up is where conversions happen

---

### When to Follow Up

👉 Start within 24 hours

---

## Step 4 — Identify Who to Message

1. Open event page
2. Click “Networking” tab
3. Review attendees
4. Identify first-level connections

---

## Step 5 — Follow-Up Message Options

### Option 1 — Offer to Register Them (Higher Conversion)

“Hi [Name], I see you’re interested in the [Event Name]. Would you like me to register you? I just need your email.”

👉 This often increases conversions significantly

---

### Option 2 — Send Registration Link

“Hi [Name], I see you’re interested in the [Event Name]. Here is the registration link: [link]”

---

### Best Practice

* Keep messages simple
* Personalize with first name
* Focus on clarity

---

## Step 6 — Team Multiplier Strategy

### How It Works

* Recruit 3–10 collaborators
* Each invites their network
* Each follows up with their connections

---

### Workflow

* Day 1 → Send invites
* Day 2–3 → Follow up
* Capture emails
* Continue follow-up

---

👉 This multiplies reach and results significantly

---

## Step 7 — CRM Capture Strategy

### Key Principle

👉 Always collect contact information when possible

---

### Why This Matters

* LinkedIn does not store usable lead data
* Email allows ongoing communication
* Supports future marketing

---

## When to Use This System

The AI should use this file when:

* The user wants to promote an event
* The user asks how to invite people
* The user wants better attendance
* The user asks about follow-up

---

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Why aren’t people registering after clicking “Interested”?**
A: LinkedIn does not automatically move users to registration. Follow-up is required.

---

**Q: How many people can I invite?**
A: Up to approximately 1,000 per week depending on account activity.

---

* Always prioritize relationship-based outreach
