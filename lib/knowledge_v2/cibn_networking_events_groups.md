# CIBN — Networking Events and Groups
## Overview
This file covers the regularly scheduled CIBN Connect networking meetings — what each group is about, who it is for, when it meets, and what to expect. The AI uses this content to help users find the right meeting for their goals and know what to expect when they attend.

---

## How CIBN Meetings Work
All CIBN Connect meetings follow a consistent workshop-style format designed to make sure every voice is heard.

**Standard meeting structure:**
- Every participant gets 60 seconds to introduce themselves — sharing who they are, what they do, and what kind of connections they are looking for
- Time per person is adjusted based on group size
- The chat is always open for communication throughout the meeting
- A host delivers curated training, a topic, or a workshop-style presentation — with little or no slides, emphasizing real conversation and sharing
- The goal of every meeting is for attendees to leave with booked appointments

All meetings are held online and are 60 minutes in length. CIBN Connect hosts over 500 events per year across all groups.

## Current Weekly Networking Groups

---

### Intros Online Networking
**When:** Every Thursday at 11:00 AM CDT
**Format:** 60 minutes, online

The entry point into the CIBN international community. This is the ideal meeting for anyone new to CIBN or to networking in general — a welcoming, practical space to learn what works, share challenges, and start building relationships with a global community.

**What happens:**
- 60-second introductions for every attendee
- Discussion of networking experiences — what's working and what the challenges are
- Workshop-style conversation with no or minimal slides
- Chat open throughout for communication and connection
- Attendees are encouraged to book meetings directly from the meeting

**Best for:** New members, guests, anyone exploring CIBN for the first time, or experienced networkers who want a consistent weekly touchpoint with the broader community.

---

### SMART Networking
**When:** Every Thursday at 3:00 PM CDT
**Format:** 60 minutes, online

A weekly deep-dive into networking best practices. New content every week covering tools, methods, and strategies that help members get measurably better results from their networking. Nothing is held back — this group shares what actually works.

**What happens:**
- 60-second introductions for every attendee
- Weekly workshop-style presentation on a specific networking topic
- Open conversation — experienced and new networkers both contribute
- Focus on implementation: attendees leave with something they can apply immediately

**Best for:** Anyone serious about improving their networking skills and getting consistent results — whether brand new or already experienced.

---

### Positively Women's Networking
**When:** Every Tuesday at 2:00 PM CDT
**Format:** 60 minutes, online

A supportive community of women entrepreneurs and business owners. Known for being genuinely friendly, encouraging, and action-oriented. This group is led by successful female entrepreneurs and creates a space for unfiltered advice, real conversation, and meaningful connections.

**What happens:**
- 60-second introductions for every attendee
- Short presentation followed by group conversation
- Appointments can be booked on the spot
- Chat open throughout

**Best for:** Women business owners and entrepreneurs at any stage who want a community that combines genuine support with practical business growth.

---

### BOSS Networking
**When:** Every Thursday at 8:30 AM CDT
**Host:** Chris Breen
**Format:** 60 minutes, online

A high-level strategy and networking meeting for serious business builders. BOSS stands for the mindset of the room — visionaries, strategists, and leaders who are building with intention. New topics every week with a focus on growth, scaling, and leadership.

**What happens:**
- 60-second introductions for every attendee
- New subject every week — healthy learning with industry-specific conversation
- Workshop-style format designed to surface expertise from the room
- Attendees are encouraged to share their professional opinions and reveal their knowledge

**Host:** Chris Breen leads this meeting weekly, bringing new topics and facilitating strategic discussion.

**Best for:** Experienced business owners, entrepreneurs, and professionals who think strategically and want to connect with others at a similar level.

## Tips for Getting the Most from Any CIBN Meeting
- Prepare your 60-second introduction in advance — know who you help, what you do, and what connections you are looking for (see the Infomercial Framework for guidance)
- Have your calendar ready to book appointments on the spot
- Use the chat actively — introduce yourself, share your contact info, and respond to others
- Attend consistently — relationships build over time, not in a single visit
- Try multiple groups to find the one that fits your energy and goals best

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Which meeting should I attend first?**
A: The Intros Online Networking meeting (Thursdays at 11:00 AM CDT) is the best starting point — it is designed for new members and guests and gives a great overview of the CIBN community.

**Q: Do I need to be a paid member to attend?**
A: Guests are welcome at CIBN events. Register through the events page at cibnconnect.com/cibn-events.

**Q: Can I attend more than one group?**
A: Absolutely. Many members attend multiple groups each week. Each meeting has a different focus and energy — try them all and find what works best for you.

**Q: What should I prepare before my first meeting?**
A: Have a clear 60-second introduction ready: who you are, what you do, and what kind of connections you are looking for. That is all you need to get started.

**Q: Are meetings recorded?**
A: Check with CIBN directly for recording availability for specific meetings.
