# CIBN — Overview

## Overview

This file introduces the CIBN system and helps users understand how everything fits together. The AI uses this to guide users toward the right next step based on what they are looking for.

---

## What Is CIBN

CIBN is a professional networking community designed to help members build strong relationships that lead to collaboration and referrals.

Instead of focusing on:

* meeting large numbers of people
* selling services

CIBN focuses on:

* building meaningful relationships
* developing collaborating partners
* creating consistent opportunities

---

## How the CIBN System Works

The CIBN system is built around a simple idea:

👉 A small number of strong relationships can create consistent business opportunities.

Members typically:

1. Meet other professionals
2. Schedule one-on-one conversations
3. Identify shared audiences and opportunities
4. Build relationships over time
5. Make introductions and referrals

---

## What Makes CIBN Different

CIBN is different from traditional networking because it focuses on:

* Relationships instead of transactions
* Collaboration instead of competition
* Consistency instead of one-time interactions

---

## The Role of Collaborating Partners

At the center of the system are **collaborating partners**

These are professionals who:

* Serve a similar audience
* Offer complementary services
* Understand each other’s value
* Introduce opportunities over time

👉 Most members focus on developing 3–5 strong collaborating partners.
