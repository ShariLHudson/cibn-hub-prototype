# CIBN — Tools & Platform

## Overview

This file explains the tools used within the CIBN networking system, how they work together, and when to use each one. The AI uses this to guide users in choosing the right tool at the right time to support relationship building, introductions, and referral tracking.

---

## How the CIBN Tools Work Together

CIBN uses several tools that each support a different part of the networking process.

👉 Simple system:

* Meet people → Meetn
* Build relationships → LinkedIn
* Identify opportunities → Chrome Extension
* Track results → Nolodex

Each tool has a specific role. Together, they support the full networking workflow.

## Trust-o-Meter
The Trust-o-Meter is an engagement tracker built into the CIBN Chrome Extension (My Most Trusted). It sits on top of LinkedIn and tracks your activity and relationship-building progress within the CIBN network.

### How It Works
As you take actions — making introductions, adding trusted connections, giving golden recommendations — you advance through five levels. Each level shows you exactly what you need to do to reach the next one, and rewards you when you get there.

### The Five Levels

**Newbie**
Your starting point. The dashboard shows you clearly what actions to take to advance to the next level.

**Junior Networker**
You get early access to the CIBN AI matching system, which connects you with your ideal prospects automatically.

**Senior Networker**
You receive an invitation to the Networkers Lounge — a private space reserved exclusively for Senior Networkers and above.

**Super Connector**
You are added to the CIBN Super Connector Hall of Fame. At this level, people begin reaching out to YOU to do business because of your reputation and reach in the network.

**Trusted Advocate**
The highest level. You become part of an exclusive group that works personally with the CIBN founder to monetize your network and take your connections to a whole new commercial level.

### Important Notes
- Every person in the CIBN network is a real human being, added one by one by members — no bots, no spam, no automation
- The Trust-o-Meter reflects your **activity level** and engagement, not just relationship strength
- Progress is visible on your dashboard at all times, with your next required action clearly displayed

## When to Use Each Tool

Use **LinkedIn** when:

* Connecting with people
* Continuing conversations
* Making introductions

---

Use **Match Me (Extension)** when:

* Looking for collaborating partners
* Searching for people connected to your audience
* Exploring referral opportunities

---

Use **Meetn** when:

* Attending networking meetings
* Joining events or training
* Meeting new professionals

---

Use **Nolodex** when:

* A referral opportunity is created
* An introduction leads to business
* You want to track results
