## About the Smart Networking Book

**Smart Networking: The $150,000 Method by Kerry George** explains how professionals can move away from traditional networking and build a more intentional, relationship-based approach to growing their business.

The book focuses on:

* Building meaningful professional relationships
* Developing 3–5 strong collaborating partners
* Creating consistent opportunities through trust and alignment

👉 It provides the deeper strategy behind how members can generate significant business results through a small number of strong relationships.

---

## What “The $150,000 Method” Means

The $150,000 Method represents the idea that:

👉 A small number of strong collaborating partners can generate meaningful revenue over time.

Instead of relying on:

* Large networks
* Constant prospecting
* Unpredictable opportunities

The method focuses on:

* Building a few high-quality relationships
* Creating consistent introductions
* Developing long-term collaboration

---

### Simple Way to Think About It

Instead of needing:

* 100+ weak connections

You focus on:

* 3–5 strong partners

👉 Who consistently introduce opportunities that lead to real business growth.
