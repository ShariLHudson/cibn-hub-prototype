# CIBN — Membership Discovery

## Overview

This file helps users explore which CIBN membership path may be the best fit for them. The AI uses this to guide users through a reflection-based conversation that clarifies how they prefer to grow their business and which membership supports that approach.

---

## Purpose of Membership Discovery

Professionals grow their businesses in different ways.

Some focus on:

* Building referral relationships
* Sharing expertise and teaching
* Turning conversations into opportunities

CIBN offers different membership paths to support each of these approaches.

👉 The goal is to help users find the best fit—not assign them to one.

---

## Membership Discovery Questions

Ask these questions one at a time.

---

### Question 1 — How You Prefer to Grow

Which feels most natural for how you like to grow your business?

* Building trusted referral relationships
* Sharing expertise and educating others
* Having meaningful business conversations that lead to opportunities
* A mix of these

---

### Question 2 — Where You Want More Opportunity

Which would you most like to strengthen?

* Receiving more introductions and referrals
* Having more opportunities to present your expertise
* Turning conversations into real business opportunities

---

### Question 3 — Your Strength Today

Which do you already do fairly well?

* Building professional relationships
* Explaining your expertise to groups
* Having one-on-one business conversations

---

### Question 4 — Where You Feel Stuck

What feels most challenging right now?

* Finding the right professionals to collaborate with
* Getting visibility for what you do
* Turning opportunities into business

---

### Question 5 — What You Want Networking to Create

If networking worked really well for you, what would it create?

* A few trusted partners who introduce opportunities
* A platform to share your expertise
* More confidence and effectiveness in conversations

---

## Membership Reflection

After all answers are provided, generate a reflection.

### Example Structure:

* Acknowledge their approach
* Highlight where they may feel stuck
* Explain how CIBN supports that
* Suggest the most aligned membership path

---

### Example Response

Based on what you shared, it sounds like building strong referral relationships and creating consistent opportunities may be important to you.

Many professionals find that while they meet interesting people, they don’t always have a structured way to turn those relationships into introductions or collaboration opportunities.

CIBN focuses on helping members develop 3–5 strong collaborating partners who serve similar audiences and can introduce opportunities over time.

You may find the Networking Membership especially helpful if your goal is to build trusted relationships that lead to referrals.
